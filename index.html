How It Works

From Booking to Release.
Every Step. Every Person.

01
Intake & Booking

Deputy arrives with a subject. Argus pre-creates the intake packet before they walk in the door. MPI lookup, nurse screening workflow, property inventory, TEK84 scan, mugshot capture, charge entry with OCGA smart search — all connected, all logged.

02
Classification & Housing

Argus drafts a classification recommendation based on charges, history, medical flags, keep-separate conflicts, and bond conditions. The Sergeant reviews and finalizes. Housing assignment enforces every rule automatically.

03
Custody Intelligence

Every event — movement, incident, court date, hold change, medical flag — flows through the event bus to every connected engine. Command staff see the full facility in real time. Nothing is siloed. Nothing is missed.

04
Constitutional Release

Release triggers an 8-layer priority stack: holds, warrants, bond conditions, mandatory holds, risk flags, administrative checks. Argus surfaces every blocking condition before the door opens. The decision is always human. The intelligence is always complete.
